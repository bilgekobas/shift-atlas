# Source Categories

## TUM Studio

Cases produced through teaching at the Technical University of Munich.

Use when the booklet or project directly provides enough case information for the atlas entry.

## TUM Studio + Atlas Editorial Supplement

Cases first identified through TUM studio work but completed through editorial review.

Use when additional information is needed, such as:

- coordinates
- climate classification
- mechanism clarification
- references
- image credits
- missing contextual information

## Atlas Curated

Cases researched and written directly by the atlas editorial team.

## Partner Studio

Cases produced by a collaborating university, seminar, or teaching partner.

## External Research

Cases based primarily on published literature, reports, databases, or expert submissions.

## Field / Community Submission

Cases grounded in field observations, interviews, community knowledge, or local documentation.

## Recommended citation note

For student-derived entries, include:

```text
Initial case identified in SHIFT studio research. Atlas entry completed through editorial review.
```
